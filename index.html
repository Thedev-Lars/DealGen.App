Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service model

How exactly does inbox access work? +

Within 24 hours of campaign launch, you'll receive login credentials for all sending inboxes used in your campaign. You'll have complete access to monitor and respond to all prospect replies. While we continue to manage the outbound campaign and technical infrastructure, you take full ownership of all responses and client relationships. This gives you direct control over the most valuable part of the process.

Do I need to check the inboxes daily? +

Yes, we recommend checking the inboxes at least once daily to ensure timely responses to interested prospects. Our system can also forward inbox responses to your primary email address so you never miss an important reply. Quick response times significantly impact conversion rates for booking meetings.

What if I don't have time to manage responses? +

While our standard service model involves clients handling responses, we do offer a premium response management add-on service for an additional fee. This service includes our team monitoring inboxes, responding to prospects according to your guidelines, and scheduling meetings on your calendar. Contact us for pricing on this premium service option.

How many emails are typically sent in a campaign? +

A standard campaign uses 3-15 sending accounts, with each account sending approximately 550 emails per month. The exact volume depends on your specific campaign requirements, target audience size, and deliverability considerations. We carefully balance volume with quality to maintain optimal inbox placement and domain health.

How personalized are the emails you send? +

We create significantly more personalized emails than typical cold email services. Our AI-powered research identifies 3-5 custom data points for each prospect, allowing us to craft messages that reference specific details about their business, role, or recent company developments. This level of personalization is a key factor in our high response rates and positive recipient sentiment.

Do I need to provide my own sending domains? +

No, we handle the entire technical infrastructure, including domain acquisition, setup, and warming. These domains are dedicated exclusively to your campaign and are properly configured with all necessary DNS records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) to ensure maximum deliverability. The cost of domain acquisition is included in your campaign resource fees.

How long does it take to see results? +

Most clients begin to see responses within the first 2-3 weeks of campaign launch. However, the full campaign effectiveness typically materializes over the 3-month initial engagement as we continually refine targeting and messaging based on response data. This is why we require a 3-month commitment—it gives us sufficient time to optimize for your specific audience and value proposition.
